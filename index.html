Our Introduction

Frontyweb’s QR Code Generator is a utility tool specially made targeting the artists allowing them to share their artworks easier and faster way with the help QR Code.


How to use the tool?

  1. Copy your artwork’s URL.
  2. Paste it in the field above
  3. Choose Foreground Color and Background Color as per your preference.
  4. Choose your QR Code’s required size ranging from 100 to 500.
  5. Click on Generate button.
  6. Your QR Code will be available.
  7. Then you can download it.

Benefits of Our QR Codes for Artists
